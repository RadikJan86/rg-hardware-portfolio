About

Electronics from prototype to real working hardware.

I am a hardware engineer with experience in electronics development, multilayer PCB design, hardware testing and system integration. My work includes warehouse automation systems, medical simulation equipment, custom electronics and embedded hardware based on STM32, AVR and ESP32 platforms.

I enjoy working with real devices: schematics, PCB layout, soldering, debugging, mechanical integration and bringing prototypes to a stable production-ready state.
